I'm Lauren Laskerr,
a web developer with Crohn's Disease.

In 2017 I was hospitalized due to Crohn's Disease. I had lost 30% of my body weight in under a month and was completely bed-ridden. Although I was ill and unable to work due to my chronic illness, my mind was still active and I wanted to figure out a way to continue to create and be productive while having more control over my life.

That led me to begin to learn how to code. While fighting my illness I began teaching myself HTML and CSS, then I moved on to JavaScript. My main goal is to use my knowledge of the healthcare field, living with chronic illness and web development to create internet things to increase patient care, access, and overall wellbeing.

I was honored to recieve a scholarship to study web development at Ironhack Miami in 2019. Game of Spoons is my 2nd project at Ironhack Miami.
