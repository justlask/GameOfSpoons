Game of Soons
The Spoon Theory

Game of Spoons is a text and choice based game exploring the reality of living with a chronic illness.

When you have a chronic illness "simple choices" or "simple tasks" can require more thought. Anyone can suffer from burnout, but when you have a chronic illness burnout can also start or worsen a flare up, which can lead to hospitalization.

Think of spoons as "energy," each day you will be given spoons to use. This amount will be random and will change every day, as some days are better/worse than others. You may choose to go "negative" and use spoons which you do not have. But be careful and mind your spoons.

Your goal is to make it through the week. Good luck!
